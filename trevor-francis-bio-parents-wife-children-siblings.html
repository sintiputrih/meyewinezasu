Trevor Francis Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth

Trevor Francis Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth: Trevor Francis, officially known as Trevor John Francis was a professional English footballer.

He developed a passion for playing football at a younger age, was consistent throughout his career, and rose to become one of the most sought-after footballers.

During his career, he played as a forward for a number of clubs in England, the United States, Italy, Scotland, and Australia, among others.

He had stints with a number of clubs including; Birmingham City, Detroit Express (NASL), Nottingham Forest, Manchester City, Sampdoria, Atalanta, Rangers, Queens Park Rangers, and Sheffield Wednesday.

In 1979, Francis hit major headlines when he became Britain’s first £1 million player following his transfer from Birmingham City to Nottingham Forest.

He won two European Cups with Nottingham Forest in 1979, when he scored the winner against Malmo, and 1980.

At international level, he played for England 52 times between 1976 and 1986, scoring 12 goals, and played at the 1982 FIFA World Cup.

Francis retired as a player in 1994, shortly before his 40th birthday, having made 632 appearances and scored 235 goals.

After handing his boots, he moved into management where he reached the finals of the FA Cup and League Cup with Sheffield Wednesday and also guided Birmingham City to a League Cup final during his time in charge.

His final managerial post was at Crystal Palace where he spent two years. Francis also worked as a media pundit with Sky Sports and BT Sport.

Sadly, the ex-England player and Britain’s first £1m footballer has died. Trevor Francis passed away at his home in Marbella, Spain on Monday, July 24, 2023. He was 69

A statement from his family said: “Trevor Francis has died at the age of 69. He had a heart attack at his apartment in Spain this morning. On behalf of the family, this has come as a huge shock to everybody. We are all very upset. He was a legendary footballer but he was also an extremely nice person.”
