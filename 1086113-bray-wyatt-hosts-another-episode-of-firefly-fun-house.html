Watch Bray Wyatt Host Another Episode Of Firefly Fun House

Last week, Bray Wyatt returned to WWE TV as the host of Firefly Fun House, a twisted take on Mister Rogers’ Neighborhood and other kids’ TV shows. This week, Wyatt hosted another episode of his program. He talked to Rambling Rabbit and called Abby the Witch a sociopath. “Take it easy, Abby. That’s all in the past, besides I’m really sorry for what I did,” said Wyatt. “That’s Abby the Witch. She’s sort of a sociopath…,” said Wyatt. “Sociopath is the word of the day.”
