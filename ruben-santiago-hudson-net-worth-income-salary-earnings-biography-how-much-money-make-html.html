Ruben Santiago-Hudson (born November 24, 1956) is an American actor and writer who has received several national accolades for his efforts in various sections of the entertainment industry. From 2009 through 2011, he starred as Captain Roy Montgomery in the ABC television series Castle. During the month of November 2011, he made his Broadway debut in Lydia Diamond’s play Stick Fly. In 2013, he appeared in the television series Low Winter Sun, which was set in Detroit and was a police procedural.
