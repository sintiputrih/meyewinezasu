Omar Rosario Boxer Wikipedia, Boxers Net Worth And Career Earnings

Omar Rosario is a six-time national amateur champion from Puerto Rico who won the 152-pound Olympic Trials in 2020. Let’s find out more about the boxer through this article.

Rosario, a 5-foot-10 boxer-puncher, also excels in the classroom. He is a marketing and finance major at Universidad Ana G. Méndez.

Omar Rosario Boxer Wikipedia: Is The Boxer Listed There?

Omar Rosario is not currently listed on Wikipedia as of now. However, with his continued dedication and hard work, we are sure Wikipedia will soon acknowledge the boxer.

However, he is very much active on Instagram and shares various information regarding his professional careers and some personal details with his fans.

According to various sources, his given name is Omar Antonio Rosario Garcia, and he hails from Puerto Rico.

Omar fought in the Superlight division and started his professional boxing career in 2020, and has not yet lost a single match.

Omar Rosario Age: How Old Is The Boxer?

Omar is currently 23 years old.

Omar was born on January 20, 1998, and will soon turn 24 years old. Rosario is from Caguas, the same city where Miguel Cotto was born. Miguel Cotto Sr., Cotto’s father, trained him as a young guy.

Omar Rosario Height: How Tall Is He?

Omar is 5 feet 10 inches or 178 cm tall, according to his profile on boxrec.com. However, his weight is not disclosed on the website as of now.

Nonetheless, the boxer is playing in the super-light division, so he should be under 140 pounds.
