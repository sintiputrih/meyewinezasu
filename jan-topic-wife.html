Jan Topić Wikipedia:- He is a businessman, economist, and politician. In the general election of Ecuador in 2023,  Jan Tomislav Topić Feraud campaigned for president. Since 2010, he has led Telconet as its president. Topić refuted the accusations of being a mercenary, despite the suspicions aroused by his purported involvement with the French Foreign Legion.
