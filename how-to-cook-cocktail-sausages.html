How do you warm up cocktail sausages?

Reheat the sausages in the oven for 10-12 minutes, turning them over halfway. Serve hot or warm, with cocktail sticks and little pots of ketchup for dipping.

How do you know when cocktail sausages are cooked?

How do you know when cocktail sausages are cooked? Heat the oil in a pan and add the cocktail sausages, cook for 2 – 3 minutes on each side until they are fully browned and cooked through. Once cooked remove the sausages from the pan and place on some kitchen paper to remove excess oil.
