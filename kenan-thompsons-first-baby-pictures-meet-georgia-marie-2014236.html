Kenan Thompson's First Baby Pictures: Meet Georgia Marie

Precious pictures! Kenan Thompson‘s wife Christina Evangeline shared two adorable snaps of their two-day-old baby daughter via Instagram on Sunday, June 22.

The Saturday Night Live star welcomed baby Georgia Marie into the world on Friday, June 20, as Us Weekly previously reported. Despite being exhausted by their new parenting schedule, proud mom Evangeline managed to find the time to post a couple of pictures of their baby daughter on the social networking site.
