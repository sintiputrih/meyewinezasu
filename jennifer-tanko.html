Jennifer Tanko Wiki (J.D. Harmeyers Wife) Age, Biography &amp; Family Fact

Jennifer Tanko (born January 6, 1988) is an American Makeup Artist, Lifestyle Blogger, and Former Teacher from Virginia, USA. She is famous for being the wife of J.D. Harmeyer (American Media Producer and Radio Personality). Furthermore, the 31-year-old Jennifer started her career as a teacher in elementary schools in Baltimore.

She was also a camp counselor at the Club Scientific (children’s science company). Moreover, Tanko also has a blog where she posts about fashion, makeup, food, fitness, and lifestyle.

Recently, the makeup artist became a yoga instructor and started her own yoga classes. As a makeup artist, Jennifer worked freelanced in Baltimore for a few years. She provided makeup services in weddings and proms. In fact, Tanko also did makeup for special effects, gore, and fantasy transformations.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

The makeup artist was born on the 6th of January 1988 in Virginia, USA. Therefore, Jennifer Tanko’s age is 31 years old, as of 2019. Her mother Linda Tanko works in the Virginia Tech Pamplin College of Business. But we have no information about Tanko’s father.

Furthermore, Jennifer completed her school education from Blacksburg High School in Virginia in 2006. The blogger graduated with a BS in sociology from the Towson University in 2010.

Moreover, she has also completed her post-graduation from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County with a masters in teaching and social studies. Later, Jennifer did an internship at the Maryland Science Center as a teacher.

Personal Affairs, Boyfriend & Spouse

Initially, Jennifer dated her ex-boyfriend Nick DiMarco. He was also a student at Towson University. Nick worked for Towerlight (campus newspaper) and is currently a news producer at Sinclair Broadcast Group.

Furthermore, she later started her relationship with her future husband J.D. Harmeyer. In late 2016, they moved in together. The love couple announced about their engagement in February 2017. They tied the knot on 25 August 2018 in Las Vegas.
