Kevin Youkilis is a former baseball player hailing from Ohio. With an estimated net worth of $24 million in 2024, Youkilis has built a successful career both on and off the field. Known for his strong performance as a first baseman and third baseman in Major League Baseball, he had a significant impact during his time with the Boston Red Sox and later with the Chicago White Sox and New York Yankees. Besides his impressive achievements in the sport, Youkilis has also ventured into various entrepreneurial endeavors, contributing to his considerable wealth.

About

3-time All-Star first baseman who helped lead the Boston Red Sox to World Series Championships in 2004 and 2007.

Before Fame

In the minor leagues he was nicknamed 'The Greek God of Walks' for his ability to get deep into the count and draw walks.

Trivia

What he lacked in power, he made up in grit: getting on base through walks, bowling over catchers at home plate, and the like.

Family Life

He married Julie Brady in 2012. His great-grandfather was Jewish and emigrated to Greece to avoid anti-semitism.

Associated With

He played alongside David Ortiz on the Red Sox from 2004 to 2012.
