Charlie Puth &amp; blackbear team up for new track 'Hard On Yourself'

Charlie Puth and blackbear have joined forces for a new track called ‘Hard On Yourself’, an anthem of positivity which we all need in our lives right now.

Bringing 90’s RnB vibes to 2020, the track features a delicate guitar melody and punchy synths whilst the pair try and lift someone who is struggling with their insecurities.

I can see you’re struggling
Promise you’ll find love again
Yeah, it will be alright
Why are you so hard on yourself?

Packed with relatable lyrics, ‘Hard On Yourself’ is a must listen and will resonate with anyone who’s ever looked at themselves unfavourably. The combination of Charlie’s soulful and blackbear’s fresh vocals is also captivating, adding a real beauty to the uplifting song.

Check out the visualiser for ‘Hard On Yourself’ below.

‘Hard On Yourself’ follows on from Charlie’s latest single ‘Girlfriend’, which has amassed over 33 million streams worldwide as well as over 8 million views on YouTube. The track also follows blackbear’s latest anthem ‘queen of broken hearts’, which is taken from his forthcoming album everything means nothing.

As well as collaborating with each other, over the past few months both Charlie and blackbear have been lending their talents to other artist projects. Charlie has been writing with the likes of John Legend, Katy Perry, 5 Seconds of Summer, and Sir Elton John, whilst blackbear has worked with Justin Bieber, G-Eazy, Marshmello, Ellie Goulding, and more.

‘Hard On Yourself’ is available to download and stream now.
