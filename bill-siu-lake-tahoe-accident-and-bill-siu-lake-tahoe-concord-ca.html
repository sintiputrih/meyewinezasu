Bill Siu Death - Remembering the Life of a Beloved Coach and Family Man

On July 29, 2023, tragedy struck when Clayton Valley Eagles coach, Bill Siu, lost his life in a drowning accident at Lake Tahoe. This heart-wrenching incident left behind his wife, Alexie, and three young boys. Bill was not just a coach but a devoted husband, father, and a remarkable human being whose life touched countless others. The Man Behind the Coach

Bill Siu was more than just a coach; he was the epitome of love, kindness, and devotion. Family meant everything to him, and he cherished his role as a husband and father. From a young age, Bill showed a keen interest in sports. At the tender age of six, he found his love for baseball, which later evolved into a passion for football at the age of eleven.
