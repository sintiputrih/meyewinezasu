What exactly is cruise control on a car?

Cruise control (also known as speed control, autocruise, or tempomat in certain countries) is a vehicle speed control system that automatically regulates the speed of a vehicle on the highway. When the driver removes his or her foot off the accelerator, the system takes over and keeps the vehicle moving at the pace set by him or her.
