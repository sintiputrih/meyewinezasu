Brody Jenner and Girlfriend Tia Blancos Relationship Timeline

Brody Jenner and Tia Blanco kicked off 2023 by announcing they were going to be first-time parents.

“To start off this new year, we’d like to take this opportunity to wish all of our friends, family and followers health, happiness & an abundance of love,” the couple, who were first linked in spring 2022, wrote via a joint Instagram post in January 2023. “We truly appreciate & love you all. We are excited to share with you the blessing of a new life in the new year.”

Prior to his romance with Blanco, Brody was in a series of high-profile relationships including Kristin Cavallari, Lauren Conrad, Nicole Richie, Jayde Nicole, Josie Canseco and Avril Lavigne. He also previously exchanged vows with Kaitlynn Carter in 2018 — but when they split in August 2019, fans learned they were never legally married.
