Us Weekly: Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom Have Grown Closer since Welcoming Their First Child

New parents Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom have reportedly grown closer, and their relationship has improved since the birth of their daughter Daisy Dove Bloom in August.

Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom are enjoying the bliss of parenthood, and the effects have spilled into their relationship. Since welcoming their beautiful daughter, Daisy Dove, things have been going smoothly for the couple.

According to an insider who spoke to Us Weekly about the family-of-three, the actor is a total hands-on dad with his two-month-old daughter and has even reduced his workload to spend more time with his baby girl.

Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom at the LA Premiere Of Amazon's "Carnival Row" at TCL Chinese Theatre on August 21, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

The insider noted that the “Pirates of the Caribbean” star is thrilled to become a father again and has kept busy with night feeding and changes as he supports Perry while she is back at work.

Neither Perry nor Bloom have shown the face of their adorable little one, and fans are eager to see the beauty.

Bloom’s joy has transitioned into his relationship with the “Dark Horse” crooner as the source added that they have grown closer since having baby Daisy. The insider explained that the couple was concerned about Perry returning to work at first, but they managed to find a way around it that has benefitted their connection.

Fans may recall that the lovebirds welcomed their first child together in August and shared the news via a heartwarming statement through UNICEF. Since her birth, little Daisy has stolen the spotlight from both her parents, with an array of celebrities sending in heartwarming messages to celebrate her arrival.

Months after her birth, Daisy maintains the same energy, and this was evident on October 11 when Bloom shared a tempting sweaty photo of him in the gym. The mirror selfie showed the award-winning actor’s killer body, but fans’ attention was focused on baby Daisy as they kept asking for photos of the adorable toddler.

Neither Perry nor Bloom have shown the face of their adorable little one, and fans are eager to see the beauty. While they patiently wait for the anticipated reveal, Perry has resumed her judging duties on season 4 of “American Idol” and hasn’t been shy to blow her fiance’s horn.

The singer lauded the actor for holding down the fort while she was gone and gushed about how adorable he looked with all the baby apparel. The songstress also revealed that she was thrilled to be back at work and feels grateful to have a meaningful job.

The new parents first crossed paths in January 2016 at a party and immediately hit things off. By April that year, they attended Coachella, and the next month made their relationship official.

The almost year-long relationship ended in February 2017 where they released a joint statement announcing their separation. More than a year after, they reconnected in May 2018 and made their first red carpet appearance by September.

In February 2019, they shocked the world with their engagement announcement and, in March 2020, shared that they were expecting baby Daisy.
