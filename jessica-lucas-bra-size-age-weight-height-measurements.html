Biography:

Jessica Lucas was born on September 24, 1985 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. She has been acting since she was seven, receiving formal training with Children’s Theatre Arts.

Her theatre work includes local productions of Snow White & The Seven Dwarfs, Grease, Cinderella, Mousetrap and Music Medley.

She made her acting debut in the television series Seven Days. In 2001, she had her on-going role as Bekka Lawrence in the Canadian teen drama series Edgemont.

She became further known for her roles in Melrose Place and Cult. She has also appeared in such films as She’s the Man, Cloverfield and Evil Dead.
