Country Music Awards 2018: Full List of Winners and Nominees

The hottest names in Nashville gathered together on Wednesday, November 14, for the 52nd Annual Country Music Awards.

Brad Paisley and Carrie Underwood hosted the ceremony for their 11th year in a row as it was broadcast live from Nashville’s Bridgestone Arena.

Chris Stapleton, who received five nominations, took the stage alongside Maren Morris and Mavis Staples during the evening. Kenny Chesney, Dan + Shay, Luke Bryan, Garth Brooks, Ricky Skaggs, Kelsey Ballerini, Keith Urban and more stars also performed.
