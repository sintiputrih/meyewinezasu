Yara Shahidis Red Carpet Style: See Her Best Outfits!

A true style star, Yara Shahidi deserves an honorary master’s degree in fashion — in addition to her undergraduate degree from Harvard University.

Shahidi’s guide to a successful outfit is simple: Comfort. “Of course, fashion is a form of expressing yourself,” she exclusively told Us Weekly in 2019. “And so I always find new pieces that speak to me, but being able to feel comfortable is everything.”

The Grown-ish alum does “the robot test” to determine if an outfit will make the cut. “If my clothes are too tight to do the robot, then I know I can’t wear them because it’s either too tight, too loose, too long or too short,” she explained.

Shahidi works with stylist Jason Bolden to come up with “vibrant and experimental” ensembles. “You can’t take yourself too seriously, especially on red carpets,” the actress told Us. “I mean, you’re going to a fun event and hearing somebody talk … it’s not life-threatening. It’s one of those moments where you are allowed to experiment with what you want to do.”
