Matt Riddles Instagram HACKED

WWE Superstars are prime targets for hackers. They are very influential and can be very helpful to achieve all sorts of scammy things online if the account falls into the wrong hands. Matt Riddle recently found himself as a victim of a hack.

The current WWE United States Champion posted to Twitter to inform fans that he’s been hacked. His Instagram is compromised, but they are working to correct the issue.

I’ve been hacked on @instagram and their support team isn’t really helping

It sounds like Riddle was able to get ahead of this problem before it got out of hand. Hopefully, the hacker wasn’t able to do a lot of damage while they had access to his account.
