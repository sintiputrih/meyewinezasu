New Lake Tahoe Hockey Team Reveals Name and Logo | News

The new ECHL hockey team coming to Lake Tahoe revealed their name and logo today.

The Tahoe Knight Monsters will play at the Tahoe Blue Events Center, where their name and logo were unveiled at a ceremony today.

The team’s colors are teal, gold and black.  The Knight Monsters team name and logo is portrayed by a dragon-like creature. The inspiration of the name and logo originated from local input and nominations from over 1000 fans.

The area has a long-standing folklore of a creature called “Tahoe Tessie,” a fictional creature that resides in Lake Tahoe.

The logos also incorporate the amazing Lake Tahoe and the mountains that border it to pay homage to the area and its world-renowned natural beauty.

“We are so incredibly excited to introduce our team’s name for our fans and for the region,” said Knight Monsters co-owner Tim Tebow, “We look forward to bringing people together for fun family entertainment, as well as making a positive impact in this community.”

The team will begin play for the ECHL’s 2024-2025 season.  A 36-home game schedule will take place at the new Tahoe Blue Event Center, a 4,200-seat arena located in Stateline, Nevada
