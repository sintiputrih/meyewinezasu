Kazuchika Okada vs Kenny Omega IV Announced, Major Details Revealed

New Japan Pro Wrestling have announced that the now legendary Kazuchika Okada vs Kenny Omega feud will have yet another chapter. The two wrestlers will square off once again, this time in a 2 out of 3 falls bout at Dominion. The previous three Omega vs Okada matches received critical acclaim and each earned Dave Meltzer’s highest 6-star recognition, as the three highest rated bouts in Meltzer’s five decade career. Last year’s Okada vs Omega Dominion match was the first ever to receive a 6.25 star rating from Meltzer. New Japan Pro Wrestling released the following video showing the latest altercation between Okada and Omega.

Omega Responds
