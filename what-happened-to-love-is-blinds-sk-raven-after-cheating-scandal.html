What Happened To Love Is Blinds SK And Raven After Their Alleged Cheating Scandal?

Highlights

  • Love Is Blind stars SK and Raven have both moved on since their split.
  • SK is passionate about investing in women in tech
  • Raven is still teaching Pilates and working as an influencer.

Netflix's Love Is Blind always has some big surprises ready for fans. Season 5 featured contestants who had previously dated and fewer engaged couples than previous seasons. But no matter how many new pairs meet in the pods and vow to love each other forever, the season 3 couples will always stand out.

Raven Ross and SK Alagbada may have gotten engaged in the pods, but from the start, their love story was different. While some fans thought that they were taking things too slowly, others approved of their timeline. No matter how people felt about them, it was a shock when Raven said that SK had cheated on her. When some people on TikTok talked about SK's alleged infidelity, things became even more dramatic. Now that a year has passed, here's what Love Is Blind's SK and Raven are up to now, and how they are faring post-split.

What Are Love Is Blind Stars SK And Raven Doing Now?

SK Alagbada doesn't seem to have dated anyone since starring in season 3 of Love Is Blind. He has been focused on finishing school.

In May 2023, SK posted a photo of himself from his University of California at Berkeley graduation on Instagram. Love Is Blind fans will remember that he was about to start working toward his MBA when he met Raven Ross in the pods. He wrote in the caption that he learned a lot about himself and grew:

"The last two years have been a roller coaster ride, but who said it was going to be easy? I have challenged myself, embraced uncertainty, tried new things, made mistakes and gained lifelong friends."

According to a post on Black Enterprise's Instagram account, SK is creating Search Fund, a private equity business. He is "passionate about investing in women in tech" and wants to find a company that is a Saas (Software as a Service) that he can run.

In an interview with the publication, SK said that while he appreciates that he became famous as a result of starring in Love Is Blind, he likes that the focus is now on his business skills and opportunities.

SK's Love Is Blind ex, Raven Ross, also seems to be thriving lately. From her Instagram account, it's clear that Raven is still teaching Pilates and also making some money as an influencer. In November 2023, she shared a recipe for oatmeal and promoted the brand Siggs.

In September 2023, Raven shared in an Instagram post that she had experienced some big life changes:

"10 months ago I was at the lowest point of my life and today I moved into my dream apartment in the best city in the world and have the healthiest relationship ever. Trust me it will get better."

According to People, the Love Is Blind star talked about her new relationship in March 2023. Just one month earlier, she explained in an interview with the publication that her boyfriend had sent her a social media message. She called it "new" but said it was a good relationship.

While Raven didn't share who her boyfriend was, some people believe they know. The Tab reported that some say she is dating Christian Crosby, who is an NBA player. This is because Raven shared a photo of herself standing with her partner and people said that Crosby also shared a social media picture with similar clothing on.

What Happened Between Love Is Blind's SK And Raven?

According to Today.com, in the late fall of 2022, Raven Ross said that SK Alagbada was unfaithful. The reality star talked about the alleged infidelity and said that she had no idea that this was going on when they got engaged for the second time.

Raven told Today.com, “Even though it’s so hard to go back and watch, I feel so blessed that you guys really got to see the bottom of our relationship, which was the pods and our wedding. That was nowhere near where we ended up. We grew together in a great way, I thought. We were able to be vulnerable with each other especially by the time we got reengaged and even after that.”

When Raven said this, SK said that he hadn't been unfaithful at all. In February 2023, SK was interviewed by People and explained his side of the story. He said that he and Raven weren't "committed" when he was chatting with other women and when he traveled to Cabo. He also said that Raven's "ex was still in the picture" during Love Is Blind season 3.

SK told People that he feels bad about what happened and that he can reflect on how he behaved:

"I do want to deeply express my sincere apology to Raven for the hurt and embarrassment that I caused her. I'm not proud of how I conducted myself. I conducted myself in a way that's making me take a step back and reflect."

While Love Is Blind stars SK and Raven seem to have different accounts of how their relationship ended, fans can be certain that they are both doing better now and they seem happy.
