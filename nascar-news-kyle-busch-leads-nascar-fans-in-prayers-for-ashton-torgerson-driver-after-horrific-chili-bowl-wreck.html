Kyle Busch Leads NASCAR Fans in Prayers for Ashton Torgerson Driver AfterHorrific Chili Bowl Wreck

Many consider the Chili Bowl a prestigious event. However, during the latest iteration of the event, saw a rather horrifying accident. Following the crash, many of the sports community stepped forward on social media to share their messages of support. A 16-year-old driver by the name of Ashton Torgerson was competing in the event when he ended up in a terrible crash. Mostly leaving fans in shock as to what they had just witnessed.

Torgerson‘s car was seen flipping up in the air several times. Furthermore, at one point, his head, along with the helmet, was visible, almost coming out of the top of the car. After another passing car hit his car, he flipped over a few more times. Finally ejecting the 16-year-old completely out of the car. Torgerson was then carried out by emergency services and was reported to be conscious and communicating with the first responders. Later on, they transported him to a local hospital.

The ambiance at the venue fell silent during the crash. Everyone prayed for the well-being of the young driver. Furthermore, drivers from the NASCAR community also sent out tweets praying for the teenage driver and his well-being. NASCAR driver Kyle Busch was one such personality to have sent out a message for the novice driver.

Moreover, Ashton’s team recently posted an update surrounding his health and how he had been following his horrific crash. As per the update shared on Twitter, it mentioned, “Passed back, neck tests waiting on CT scan… 1st thing he asked me was what place was he in!!..”
