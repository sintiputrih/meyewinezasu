Giannis Antetokounmpo Height

Giannis Sina Ugo Antetokounmpo, known professionally as Giannis Antetokounmpo, is a popular Greek professional basketball player for the Milwaukee Bucks of the National Basketball Association.

Giannis Antetokounmpo has been nicknamed “Greek Freak” due to his size, speed, strength, and ball-handling skills.

One of the youngest NBA players ever, Antetokounmpo made his league debut on October 13, 2013, when he was 18 years, 311 days old. 

In 77 games during his first season, he averaged 6.8 points, 4.4 rebounds, 1.9 assists, 0.8 steals, and 0.8 blocks.

Giannis Antetokounmpo Height

Giannis Antetokounmpo is indeed very tall. He stands at a height of around 7 feet tall. He weighs about 243 lb or 110 kg.
