Does Rickey Smiley live in Alabama? Where is Rickey Smiley now?

Rickey Smiley is an American stand-up comedian, television host, actor, and radio personality.

Broderick Dornell Smiley, professionally known as, Rickey Smiley was born on August 10, 1968, in Birmingham, Alabama, United States. His mother is Carolita Smiley Lester. He is best known for his prank phone calls where he disguises his voice.

Rickey Smiley’s stand-up comedy is distinguished by his remarkably clean delivery and the wide range of humorous personalities he employs in his act. “Bernice Jenkins,” “Rusty Dale,” and “Beauford” are among them. Smiley was hired as a morning radio host by Dallas, TX radio station KBFB in 2004.

Rickey Smiley is the host of the nationally syndicated Rickey Smiley Morning Show based in Atlanta from its flagship affiliate WHTA “Hot 107.9”. He has starred in the sitcom The Rickey Smiley Show, which aired on TV One and is also a featured columnist on the Fox-produced tabloid nationally-syndicated show Dish Nation.

Rickey hosted ComicView during its first season in 2000. During this time, he also appeared on other TV shows such as Showtime at the Apollo, Def Comedy Jam, and Uptown Comedy Club, where he did stand-up comedy.

In 2015, Rickey Smiley started appearing on Rickey Smiley For Real, a reality television series about his life. Rickey Smiley has also appeared in several comedy films, including Ice Cube’s Friday After Next and the similarly titled but unrelated First Sunday, in which he plays his character Bernice Jenkins.

Does Rickey Smiley live in Alabama?

Yes, Rickey Smiley lives in Birmingham, Alabama.

Where is Rickey Smiley now?

Rickey Smiley is now the host of the nationally syndicated Rickey Smiley Morning Show ( G93 – WMPZ FM) based in Atlanta.
