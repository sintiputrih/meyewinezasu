Happy New Year star seeds.

As we burn what’s been and look forward to what will be we do so under the light of the first full moon of 2023.

The Wolf Moon in Cancer arrives on Friday January 6th, reaching its prime and peak opportunity to howl, at 6:08 P.M. ET.

In terms of numerology, 2023 is a seven and the tarot card associated is The Chariot. This card represents the need to gain command over our emotions in order to navigate thoughtfully and progressively in our relationships with others and on our path to personal actualization.

This paradigm becomes increasingly important under a Cancer full moon when emotions run high and our proverbial feels run deep, deep, deep. Cancer is ruled by the moon and when the full moon falls in this sign we are at our moodiest and moon-iest. Think belly aches, blue notes and increased nipple sensitivity.

Ruling the fourth house of home and root systems, Cancer is the most nostalgic sign in the zodiac, a bit of a blessing and a bane depending on where and how you cast your reflective lens. Idealizing a lost relationship? Candy coating your childhood? Self-destructive wistfulness? These tendencies might pacify us in the present moment but serve only and ever to paralyze our progress. This courting of what has been is enhanced by the ongoing influence of Mercury’s retrograde in Capricorn, itself a time of backspinning and a return/retreat to the intangible past.

Yet, while this retrograde and this lunation ask us to look back they encourage us to do so with the clear intention of building from it, rather than longing for it. Full moon in Cancer 2023

The full moon in Cancer coincides with the sun shining in boot strapping, belt tightening, legacy building Capricorn. In astrology, Capricorn and the sun are synonymous with the father while Cancer and the moon are associated with the archetype of the mother. When these parental luminaries are in cahoots, our childhoods, the wounds we incurred during them and the insecurities we carry out of them are high on the horizon.

Yet, we are also presented with the divine opportunity to reparent ourselves. With every new year we are newborn and as such get to choose how and with what degree of tenderness and structure we raise ourselves. My preferred exercise for this lunation is to write a list of what you needed as a child and weren’t given (don’t show your mom) and see how and if you can give yourself those things in some literal or metaphorical way this year.

How can we take responsibility for reparenting and fulfulling ourselves and how can this emotional autonomy prepare us to love others from a place of desire rather than desperation? Heavy themes but ones that set the tone for the year to come.

What is the spiritual meaning of the Wolf Moon in 2023

January’s full moon is known as the wolf moon as it is believed the howling song of these pack predators can be heard most clearly in the earliest month of the year. The spiritual symbolism of the wolf again recalls lessons of autonomy, communion and actualization. The term “lone wolf” is a bit of a misnomer as wolves are pack animals that strike out on their own not because they prefer solitude but rather because they seek something more, somewhere beyond; to find a mate, their own territory or to form a pack of their own.

I encourage you to consider the modality of the wolf under the light of this moon. If you have bound yourself to an ancestry, blood line, relationship or family tie that is limiting you in any way you have the right and the responsibility to retreat into the company of yourself with the express purpose of finding out and firming up what you need. Ther pack, place and partner you deserve await you on the other side of this wilderness. This moon is about howling to who you want to be and listening closely to the echo of your future self.

This full moon pushes the bruises and buttons of your fourth house of home and ancestry asking you to consider the ways the past, and past versions of yourself are preventing you from moving forward. If all of your elders have failed you, channel an ancestor you’ve never met and move through the world with their history and healing it, as your fuel.

The wolf moon in your third house of communication means that you might be tasked with talking about, rather than brooding over, your feelings my bull babies. Rabble rousing punk rock Uranus in your sign forms a sextile to the moon and indicates that the way you relate to information and relay it to people may change wildly and for the better. This newfound language might shock you but trust, its a jolt in the right direction.

This full moon in watery Cancer is bringing to the surface resentments and attachments of the financial variety. Do you feel unappreciated on the job front? Are you putting in more than you’re getting out in terms of energy and income? Figure out what being seen and feeling financially secure look like for you and then trust in your divine right to both.n

This full moon is a particularly introspective one for you lions. The moon is sending its silver into your twelfth house of the unconscious mind and spiritual inclinations. In all likelihood the past few weeks have been a flurry of social activity and energetic exchanges. The time is nigh for retreat and reflection. Have yourself a cat nap and a hard reset.

This full moon falls in your eleventh house of friends and community. Given the influence of this Mercury retrograde you could see a reemergence of old ghosts rather than new faces. Proceed with caution but also deep appreciation, interacting with people from your past can serve to remind you how far you’ve come from who you once were. But take heed, no backsliding bull***t allowed.

The full moon in Cancer shines its moody light on your tenth house of career and public image. This lunation is a time to discern who you are and how you wish to be remembered apart from what is expected of you and who you are trying to please, Libra. Professional success without personal fulfillment is like a candle with no wick.

This full moon sees you coming out of the proverbial long night of the soul and into the strange shores of new adventure and expanded thought. Whether your journeys are of the literal or metaphorical variety the time is nigh to take the first step. Be open to having your mind blown and your maps burned.

This lunation is pouring moonlight on your shadows and your eighth house of sex, death, and regeneration.
