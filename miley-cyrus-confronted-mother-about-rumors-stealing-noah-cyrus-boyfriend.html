Miley Cyrus Confronted Her Mother About The Rumors Of Her 'Stealing' Noah Cyrus' Boyfriend

Highlights

  • Noah Cyrus was allegedly involved with her mother's new husband before they started dating, creating tension in the family.
  • Miley Cyrus confronted her mother about the rumors and found herself stuck in the middle of a complicated situation.
  • Tish Cyrus and Dominic Purcell are happily married, but the controversial beginnings of their relationship have caused turmoil within the family.

It's always hard to be the person stuck in the middle of a feud, and for Miley Cyrus it's especially difficult, considering the people fighting are her mother and her sister. The Cyrus family has been going through a hard time since Billy Ray and Tish Cyrus' divorce, which strained Miley's relationship with her dad drastically. With Noah Cyrus skipping Tish and Dominic Purcell's wedding, it seemed the children had chosen sides, but it seems the problems are more serious than that.

Tish Cyrus Reportedly Pursued Her New Husband While He Was Dating Daughter Noah

Sources have now shared that there is another reason why Noah Cyrus disapproves of her mother's new marriage.

Rumor has it, Noah Cyrus had been seeing her mother's husband right before they started dating, and had a hard time forgiving Tish Cyrus for "stealing" her boyfriend. Miley allegedly found out about this drama at the same time fans did, and has been having a hard time deciding what to do.

Miley Cyrus Confronted Her Mother About The New Rumors

Miley Cyrus most likely knew that whatever happened between her sister Noah and their mother had to be big. After all, the singer was the one that hosted Tish Cyrus' wedding to Dominic Purcell, which Noah Cyrus skipped.

However, according to new reports, Miley had no idea of the alleged love triangle between Noah, Tish, and Dominic. And she didn't take things lightly once she found out.

"Miley had no idea about the drama with Dominic. No idea at all," a source stated about it. It was a very uncomfortable situation for Miley Cyrus, who is now stuck in the middle of a fight between two people she loves.

"She confronted her mom about it. She thinks it’s a strange situation, but she loves her mom and wants her to be happy," the source added.

The fact remains that Dominic Purcell and Tish Cyrus are happily married and in love, and after the traumatic divorce her mother went through, it makes sense that Miley wouldn't want to disturb her happiness despite the controversial way the relationship allegedly began.

Noah Cyrus And Dominic Purcell Were Seeing Each Other Before He Started Dating Her Mother

The Cyrus family feud reached new levels of controversy when reports came out about the complicated beginnings of Tish Cyrus and Dominic Purcell's relationship.

Noah Cyrus made her disapproval of her mother's new marriage public by pointedly skipping her wedding, but until recently, everyone thought it was out of loyalty to her father, Billy Ray. When they married in August last year, Miley Cyrus was already at odds with her dad, so when Noah posted a photo of herself with a Billy Ray Cyrus shirt while the wedding was happening, fans thought it was a matter of choosing sides.

However, sources are now claiming the rift between Noah and Tish Cyrus stems from a past romantic relationship between Noah and her mom's new husband.

Noah Cyrus’ Feud With Her Mom Could Ruin Tish’s New Marriage To Dominic Purcell

With allegations that Noah was romantically involved with Dominic Purcell before he got with her mom, things aren't looking good.

Apparently, Dominic was dating Noah when Tish first approached him, and the mother was reportedly aware of the relationship when she decided to pursue him.

"Noah and Dominic were seeing each other in a friends with benefits way, off and on. They stopped seeing each other and then Tish started something up," a source said, adding that "Tish knew he had been seeing Noah."

Despite the fact that the relationship wasn't serious and had already ended when Tish and Dominic started going out, Noah was "very offended" by their behavior, especially because "Tish never gave Noah the chance to talk about all of this before they got married."
