Who Is Ingrid Hunter? Meet Coach Kenneth Hunter Wife!

Meet Ingrid Hunter, wife of Netflix’s Last Chance U coach Kenneth Hunter. Are they still married? How many kids do they have? What does she do for a living? Where is she from?

Meet Ingrid Hunter, Coach Kenneth Hunter Wife

On 6 June 2014, coach Kenneth Hunter gave a glimpse into his relationship when he posted on his Facebook, “Up thinking how blessed a brother is, screaming HAPPY 17th damn did I say 17th Anniversary to my wife Ingrid Nicole Hunter!!! Been together for 20 yrs, more than half my life, and still going!!! Can’t be there today but I’ll be on the beach with you tomorrow night, love you and Stay Blessed!!! I’m bumping the intro of course with a little Tony Toni Tone.”

This means they have been married for 25 years as they tied the knot on 6 June 1997. Later in August 1997, they had a huge wedding which is why they celebrated two anniversaries every year. They were in a relationship for three years before their wedding.

However, June 2021’s anniversary was likely the last wedding anniversary for the couple. According to February 2022 post, “We had a good 28 yr run(since Dec 94) and lord knows I tried. I’ve been blessed w 4 beautiful kids 2 grown, 1 who just graduated college, 1 on his own raising his family working and still in college, one great amazing 15yr old, and one amazing 8 yr old who helps extend our time together.”

He continued writing, “People grow apart, I’m not the same guy who got time to put up w self-sabotage. I can’t feel sorry for anybody cause I don’t even feel sorry for myself. Life happens you have to learn to keep grinding and keep it moving. Wish her nothing but the best and hope she continues to be a good mother to our kids.”

On Mother’s Day 2021, Ken wished his wife via an IG post. He wrote, “VERY HAPPY MOTHERS DAY TO ALL THE LADIES ESPECIALLY THESE TWO WHO HAVE ALLOWED ME TO EXPERIENCE SO MANY HIGHS IN LIFE, THANK YOU!!!.”

Coach Kenneth Hunter And Ingrid Hunter Children

Coach Kenneth and his wife Ingrid Hunter shared four children together. They are Tiana, Kenneth III, Ian, and Quen.

Tiana was a Californian who lived in Los Angeles. She turned 24 years old in March 2022. She was a student at Melvindale High.

Kenneth Jr. was a psychology student at the University of Michigan at the same time. Later, he was employed by Diamond Supply Co. Kenneth Jr is a former manager at Diamond Supply Co. He studied Sport psychology at the University of Michigan. He attended phantom Basketball – Cathedral High School – Los Angeles, CA.

Though the rest of the children appear on Ken’s socials, not much is known about them.

As the post mentioned earlier about Ken and Ingrid’s separation, the youngest two children Ian and Quen are 15 and 8 years old respectively.

What Is Ingrid Hunter Maiden Name?

The maiden name of Ingrid Hunter is Baxter. She was born Ingrid Nicole Baxter to her parents Verlestine Baxter and Quentin Baxter.

Ingrid Hunter Job

Ingrid Hunter wears many hats as she is a Writer, Singer, Actor, Social Media Influencer, and “soon-to-be best-selling author.” According to her LinkedIn, she has been in the customer service industry for more than 20 plus years.

She writes in her LinkedIn bio, “with a background in Business, Entertainment, Marketing, and Technical Support. I have customer service experience and training from Apple Inc and Disney. With previous experience in customer service as CNA 20 years, Technical Support Specialist 1 year, Cast Member 2 years, and Eligibility Technician 8 years.”

Ingrid is currently working at the County of Orange as an Employment and Eligibility Specialist. She took the job in February 2022. Before that, she was working as an Eligibility Technician since May 2013.

Ingrid briefly worked at Apple Inc. as a technical support specialist. She had also worked at FedEx Supply Chain Services Inc as a data entry specialist.

Talking about her education, Ingrid attended University of Redlands School of Business & Society from where she secured her MSOL, Business Administration and Management, General in 2020. She earned her BA in Communication and Media Studies from California State University.

At Fullerton College, Ingrid studied Business/Interdisiplinary Studies, Entertainment and Tourism. She also has Master of Social Work from Grand Canyon University.
