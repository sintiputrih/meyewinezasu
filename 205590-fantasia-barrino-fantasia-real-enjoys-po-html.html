Fantasia Barrino of 'Fantasia for Real' Enjoys Pool Time with Family in New Photos

Fantasia Barrino soaks up the sun and enjoys some time by the pool with her family in a new update for her fans on Instagram amid self-quarantine.

Fantasia Barrino enjoyed some fun under the sun with her boys, King, Dallas, and Leo amid their self-quarantine efforts due to COVID-19. The family enjoyed some pool time as their little puppy watched, and it looked like a fun-filled activity for everyone.

Fantasia donned a gorgeous pink bikini as she waded by the pool, enjoying time with her boys. Meanwhile, her 18-year-old daughter Zion chose not to join their outdoor fun as she didn't want to mess up her hair.

Singer Fantasia Barrino attends the 2016 BET Awards at Microsoft Theater on June 26, 2016 | Photo: Getty Images/GlobalImagesUkraine

HER ELDEST DAUGHTER ZION

Zion was introduced to the world back in 2004 when Fantasia won "American Idol," and at the time, she was only about three-yeas-old. Now, Zion is a beautiful 18-year-old girl who is enjoying legality to the fullest.

During her birthday greeting, Barrino reminisced about giving birth to her darling daughter at such a young age, until their precious memories together while she was growing up. In the end, the proud mom had nothing more to say but for her daughter to "go be great for mama."

PRACTICING SOCIAL DISTANCING

Now, Fantasia and her family are at home amid the stay-at-home orders put in place due to COVID-19. They are doing their part in trying to stop the spread of the highly-contagious virus, and they've tried to keep themselves busy through it all.

Luckily for the family, they have their own backyard that they can enjoy, which gives them the opportunity to go outdoors without having to leave their house. When they do head out for walks, they make sure to practice social distancing wherever they go.

SPENDING TIME TOGETHER AMID QUARANTINE

Recently, Tasia posted a picture of Zion by a stream, as they enjoyed a quiet hike without any other people with them. Fans noted in the comment section that her first-born was growing up to be such a beautiful girl, and how she's growing up so fast.

Fantasia loves her family dearly, and having to stay at home has been an opportunity for them to spend time together given her otherwise tight schedule when she has to head out to work.
