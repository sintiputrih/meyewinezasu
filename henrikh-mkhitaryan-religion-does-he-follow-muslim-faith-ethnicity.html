Henrikh Mkhitaryan Religion: Does He Follow Muslim Faith? Ethnicity

The admirers are keen to know about Henrikh Mkhitaryan’s faith and what religion does he follow? He is a Christian devotee.

Henrikh Mkhitaryan is also known as Heno (the short form of Henrikh in Armenia) and Micki internationally.

Heno is an Armenian professional soccer player who plays attacking midfielder or winger for Inter Milan of Serie A.

Micki started his senior career with Pyinik and won four Armenian Premier League titles before moving to Metalurg Donetsk in 2009.

The professional midfielder joined Shakhtar Donetsk in 2001 and was named the Footballer of the Year for the 2012–13 season.

He joined Manchester United in 2016, becoming the first Armenian to play in the Premier League. With United, he won the FA Community Shield and the UEFA Europa League title.

Besides, Mkhitaryan signed with the Italian team Inter Milan in 2022 after playing with Arsenal and Roma.

Henrikh Mkhitaryan Religion And Ethnicity

Playing in one of the top soccer leagues, fans show their interest in their favorite player’s personal information. They have also indicated concerns about what religion Henrikh follows.

According to his Instagram post, he was seen celebrating Christmas with his wife and son.

Also, Henrikh took his vows in the church in front of Jesus Christ, which indicates he is a committed Christian.

On January 6, 2021, he also made a post on Instagram with a caption that read, “Today Armenians celebrate their Holy Christmas, which also coincides with the Epiphany.”

Hence, this all indicates Mkhitaryan follows Christianity as his religion.

Talking about his ethnicity, Henrikh belongs to Armenian-white by ethnicity. His father was from Armenia, whereas the player’s mother was from Moscow, Russia.

Henrikh Mkhitaryan Family

Henrikh was born on January 21, 1989, in Yerevan, Armenia, to Hamlet Mkhitaryan and Marina Taschyan.

His father, Hamlet, was also an Armenian soccer player who played for FC Ararat Yerevan in the 1980s.

Additionally, Hamlet played as a striker for the Armenian National Team. However, he died at age 33 due to a brain tumor.

At the time of his death, Henrikh was about 7 years old. Besides, Henrikh grew up with an older sister, Monica, who works at the UEFA headquarters.

After the demise of his father, Micki’s mother raised two kids as a single mother.

Marina Taschyan is currently the head of football operations in the Armenian Football Federation.

As a child, Henrikh was greatly influenced by his father’s soccer career and has been passionate about the sport since childhood.

Following in his father’s footsteps, he is playing at one of the top soccer leagues in the world and making a name for himself.

Furthermore, Mkhitaryan is a married man. He tied the knot with Betty Vardanyan at the monastery of San Lazzaro degli Armeni near Venice, Italy, in June 2019.

After a year, they gave birth to a beautiful son on March 4, 2020, and named him Hamlet Mkhitaryan to honor Henrikh’s father.
