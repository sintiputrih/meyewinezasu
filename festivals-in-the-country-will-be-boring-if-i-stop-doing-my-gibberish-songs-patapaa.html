Festivals In The Country Will Be Boring If I Stop Doing My Gibberish Songs Patapaa

Justice Amoah better known in Showbiz as Patapaa has stated that he’s the pioneer of gibberish song in Ghana.

He explained that creating gibberish song is his hobby and no one can claim his tittle of being the originator in Ghana.

According to him, every artiste who will follow his style of music will be associated to his him especially the underground artiste.

The Swedru base artiste emphasized that his songs are the reason in the country became entertaining and enjoyable.

Patapaa noted that he relaxed on creating gibberish songs because of the global pandemic but once restrictions are totally ease people will witness what will happen in the dance space of the country.

In an interview on GBC Radio Central, he added that gibberish song brought his breakthrough hence won’t stop making such songs because festival will be boring because he make people move with his songs.
