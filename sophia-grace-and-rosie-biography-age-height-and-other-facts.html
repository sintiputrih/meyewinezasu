Sophia Grace And Rosie Biography, Age, Height and Other Facts

Sophia Grace and Rosie are British child cousins ​​who rose to fame in 2011, aged 8 and 5, after the cover of famous singer and rapper Nicki Minaj’s song Super Bass. Also, they gained popularity when they appeared on the Ellen DeGeneres show where they met Nicky Minaj and later became regular guests of Ellen on her show.

Sophia Grace and Rosie’s Bio and Age

Looking at the background of these Youngsters would find that Sophia Grace, the eldest of the duo, was born with the birth name Sophia Grace Brownlee on April 18, 2003, while her younger cousin, Rosie, was born with the birth name Grace McClelland. , born September 7, 2006. The girls who are originally from Essex, England are duo Sophia Grace & Rosie. In September 2011, their mother, Danielle, dressed the girls in ballerina outfits with tiaras on their heads and made a video of them to Nicki Minaj’s song “Super Bass,” which she posted on YouTube. The video immediately went viral with over 53 million views as of now and it has reportedly caught the attention of many Hollywood celebrities. In October 2011, the girls were invited to participate in theEllen DeGeneres Show of Ellen Herself where they had the opportunity to Nicky Minaj in person.

The duo’s celebrity status gradually improved, as they made regular television appearances. The Ellen DeGeneres show and even had a segment for their personal show called Tea Time with Sophia Grace & Rosie in which they guest stars like Miley Cyrus, Taylor Swift, Katy Perry, Hugh Grant, Justin Bieber, Julie Bowen, Harry Connick Jr, LL Cool J and Reese Witherspoon. They also reportedly represented Ellen DeGeneres at official occasions and won the 2012 Teen Choice Award given to ‘Choice Web Star’. The pair were featured in two episodes of the sitcom Sam & Cat and have their own movie called Sophia Grace and Rosie’s Royal Adventureproduced by Nickelodeon which would have other series to follow.

In 2013, the girls published 2 books via OrchardBooks Illustrated by Shelagh McNicholas. The first titled “Tea Time with Sophia Grace & Rosie” was released on February 1, 2013, and was ranked number two among children’s illustrated books on the New York Times Bestseller list. Their second book ‘Showtime with Sophia Grace & Rosie’, released on March 25, 2014, also fared very well like the first.

Sophia Grace and Rosie also have a line of dolls launched in April 2014. The dolls are a set of two representing girls and were only sold at Walmart in April, but in May they were found in Claire’s store. The dolls are unique as they were dressed in the typical feminine look which was a pink tutus with tiaras on their heads, microphones, socks and shoes. The dolls even sing Nicki Minaj’s “super bass” when their tummy is squeezed,

Sophia Grace who is a music lover released her first music video in 2013 titled “Girls Just Gotta Have Fun” and in 2015 she released the single “Best Friends” which got over 118 million views on her YouTube channel. She was reportedly signed to Capitol Records France and released a third single titled “Girl In The Mirror” on June 9, 2016 in which she featured rapper Silentó. Rosie, on the other hand, has also worked hard like her cousin, as she has her own YouTube channel and sang a cover of the Little Mix song, “Touch”.
