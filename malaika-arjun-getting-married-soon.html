Malaika Arora and Arjun KapoorMalaika &amp; Arjun Getting Married Soon?

Malaika Arora got separated from her husband Arbaaz Khan in the year 2017. She married him in the year 1998 and they have been together for 18 years. After getting divorced from Arbaaz, Malaika has been together with Arjun Kapoor and they are undoubtedly the latest and hot pair of Bollywood, making headlines every now and then. Fans of Malaika have been wanting to know when she is going to marry Arjun and finally, the news is here. Till now, there has been no news or a statement from Boney Kapoor, father of Arjun regarding the latter’s marriage with the Bollywood diva. An official confirmation from the couple is awaited.
