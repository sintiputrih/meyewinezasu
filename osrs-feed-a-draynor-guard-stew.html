OSRS Feed a Draynor Guard Stew

If you don’t know where to find OSRS Draynor Guard and how to feed a Draynor Guard some stew, you are at the right place. This task is part of the new Trailblazer Reloaded League 4 tasks. The new League number 4 started on November 15th, 2023, and will be available for 8 weeks. In this guide we’ll show you where to find the Draynor Guard and how to give him some OSRS Stew.

OSRS Feed a Draynor Guard Some Stew

OSRS Feed a Draynor Guard Some Stew

If you don’t know where OSRS Draynor Guard is, we can direct you toward the Draynor Village Bank. You can find him just to the west of the Draynor Village bank. There’s a tree with a guard hidden within its branches. This guard is on a side mission to monitor a suspect involved in a bank robbery. Once you find him you can offer him a stew, and he will reward you with 30 coins. After a short while, he will drop a bowl near the tree. Please be aware that players are not able to offer him another stew until he has dropped the bowl from the tree.

However, if you try to chop this tree you’ll get no logs, but the guard will start protesting that you’ll blow his cover and draw attention to him. This process requires a Cooking level of 25 and there’s a chance that players might accidentally burn the stew.
