Pregnant Woman Dances Until Her Water Breaks: Video

Who would have thought the water breaking would be the least surprising part? A new viral video is making the rounds of a heavily pregnant woman getting her groove on until her water breaks on the kitchen floor. 

Though the YouTube clip was originally posted in May 2014, it is recently gaining traction online, reaching close to 3 million views. 

It begins with a man videotaping the pregnant woman (whose name has yet to be revealed) in the kitchen, and saying, “Girl, you know you’re too big to be standing up there, you need to sit down.” 

“Just turn the song on bro, it’s an emotional time for me,” she replies before swaying her hips to the beat of Sade’s “No Ordinary Love.”
