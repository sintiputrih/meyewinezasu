Drew Brees Teaches 'Average Andy' How to eFoil in Hilarious 'Ellen' Skit!

Drew Brees is giving Andy Lassner a crash course in eFoiling!

The Super Bowl MVP and former New Orleans Saints quarterback stopped by The Ellen DeGeneres Show on Tuesday (June 1) to teach Ellen‘s producer a lesson in eFoiling, a form of surfing that uses a remote-controlled electric surfboard.

Click inside to see the video…

In the clip, Drew helps Andy out with his wetsuit before the pair enter the water to try out their surfboards.

“Andy, I’m just trying to give you a little moment of inspiration there,” Drew says as he impressively rides a wave.

“I have twenty Emmys, Drew,” Andy quips in response. “Don’t you forget it.”
