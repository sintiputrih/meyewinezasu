About

Perhaps most well known for her role as Tammy in the 2014 Chris Rock comedy feature Top Five, Norman is also recognized for her work in the teen movie Fired Up! (2009); the Forest Whitaker and America Ferrera starrer Our Family Wedding (2010); and the television shows Hello Cupid (2013-14) and Selfie (2014).

Before Fame

She trained at both the Upright Citizens Brigade and the American Academy of Dramatic Arts (both in Los Angeles, California) and went on to appear as a briefcase model on the NBC quiz show Deal or No Deal.

Trivia

A popular web comedienne, she wrote and acted in several viral Funny or Die videos.

Family Life

She spent her youth in Southern California and is an animal rights advocate and a vegan.

Associated With

In 2008, she appeared with Dennis Hopper in two episodes of the Starz series Crash.
