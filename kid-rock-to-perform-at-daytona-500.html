Kid Rock To Perform At Daytona 500

DAYTONA BEACH, FL (CelebrityAccess) — Kid Rock has been tapped to perform at a pre-race concert at NASCAR's Daytona 500 in February.

A reputed fan of NASCAR, the Kid will perform several of his hits, including his brand new single "French Kiss" from his latest album on pit road ahead of the start of the pre-season opener of the Nascar Sprint Cup Series on February 22nd.

“We’re thrilled to have Kid Rock entertain the thousands of race fans in attendance and the millions more watching on FOX,” Daytona International Speedway President Joie Chitwood III said. – Staff Writers
