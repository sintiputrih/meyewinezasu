Monster Hunter Rise Sunbreak - How To Unlock All Switch Skills

Here's a guide that will show you how to get all new Switch Skills in Monster Hunter Rise Sunbreak expansion.

First Published July 18, 2022, 15:10

The newly released Monster Hunter Rise Sunbreak expansion that was released 30th June is slowly coming to its endgame. As such veterans of this MMORPG are eager to unlock and uncover each new feature this expansion has to offer, including new Rampage Decorations. 

Switch Skills has been a great asset to the game with its customization and diverse abilities available for combat. The latest feature of the expansion is the Switch Skill Swap mechanic. In this article, we’ll cover how to unlock this mechanic in the new MHR Sunbreak expansion.

MHR: Sunbreak - How to Unlock all Switch Skills

In Monster Hunter Rise Sunbreak, you’ll need to make your way to Elgado for the first time, after you have defeated a Daimyo Hermitaur in the Shrine Ruins, you’ll find that Elgado’s Arena Master Arlow would like to have a chat with you regarding the new Switch Skill mechanic in Sunbreak called Switch Skill Swap. 

This mechanic allows you to switch out your preloaded Switch Skills easily throughout your hunts. It's Master Utsushi who will give you a number of new Switch Skills for each weapon when you accept an Arena Quest where he will teach you how to use this new ability. Although, it will only be a portion of it. 

However, the best Switch Skills will only be available once you’ve reached Master Rank 4 and completed two Urgent Quests. Players will then receive a quest that requires them to talk to Master Utsushi in Kamura Village Gathering Hub, this is when you will be given the remaining 28 Switch Skills. The total amount of Switch SKills in Monster Hunter Rise Sunbreak expansion is 42.
