Eugene Levy Is the Father of Two Beautiful Kids Meet Both of Them

Actor and comedian Eugene Levy is a doting father of two children who took after him and made their mark in the entertainment industry. Learn more about the famous Levy siblings below.

Eugene Levy is a father to Daniel Levy and Sarah Levy, whom he shares with his wife, Deborah Divine. Just like their dad, Daniel and Sarah are actors too.

Daniel, being the oldest child, is the spitting image of his father. On March 9, he took to Instagram to share a striking photo of him donning a classy suit.

Eugene Levy attends the Screen Actors Guild Awards | Photo: Getty Images

MEET DANIEL & SARAH LEVY

He captioned his post: "A few more from last night. Because we live for drama.” The 37-year-old shared two black and white snapshots showing regal postures.

Daniel wore an all-black two-piece ensemble. The outfit had military stars on it, and he completed the look with glasses and a wristwatch.

On the other hand, his sister also posted a lovely snapshot on the platform on New Years'. She wrote, "To hugging, kissing and squeezing all our loved ones sometime in 2021."

The shot showed her beaming while her significant other kissed her on the cheek. The couple wore black coordinated outfits, she donned a strapless dress, and he opted for a black suit.

WORKING WITH DAD

Not only are Sarah and Levy in showbiz together, but they have also had the pleasure of working with their dad. According to Closer Weekly, Eugene loves working with his kids.

The trio works together on the TV sitcom "Schitt’s Creek.” Eugene reportedly told the outlet that he is very strict with his children growing up. The 74-year-old revealed he used to ground them whenever they acted up:

“If you crossed the line, there was going to be some sort of punishment. It’s the way I was brought up, and it’s the way I brought up my kids.”

WHAT EUGENE THINKS OF THEM

Back in 2017, he got candid about their working relationship on screen. At the time, he attended The Women’s Cancer Research Fund event and revealed to Bustle how surreal the show is for him because of how blown away he is by them.

Eugene admitted that working closely with Daniel and Sarah every day was a weird experience but an incredible one, nonetheless. At the time, he shared he was taken aback by how the pair conduct themselves on set and behind the scenes.

EUGENE AND HIS WIFE’S RELATIONSHIP

Last month, Eugene received his 2020 Emmy award for his starring role in the comedy series and thanked his wife Divine during his acceptance speech. The producer thanked his spouse for showing him love, support, and sage counsel over the years.

According to Good Housekeeping, the couple started dating in 1973 and walked down the aisle in 1977 in front of 300 guests. Six years after their nuptials their welcomed their one and only son Daniel. In 1986 they welcomed their only daughter Sarah.
