Sherri Saum Net Worth

Sherri Saum is an American actress born on October 1, 1974 in Dayton, OH. She is best known for her roles as Vanessa Hart on the soap opera Sunset Beach from 1997 to 1999, Natalie on FX's Rescue Me, and Lena Adams on the ABC Family series The Fosters.
