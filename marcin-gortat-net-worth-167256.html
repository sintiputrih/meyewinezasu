Marcin Gortat Net Worth

Marcin Gortat is a Polish professional basketball player who was born on February 17, 1984 in Lodz, Poland. He was drafted by the Phoenix Suns in 2007 and made his NBA debut with the Orlando Magic. He rejoined the Suns from 2010 to 2013 before being traded to the Washington Wizards in 2013. He is nicknamed "The Polish Hammer" due to his physical style of play.
