Matt Canada Net Worth: How Much Does the Coach Earn?

Matt Canada is an American football coach. Prior to this, he worked for Butler, Indiana, Wisconsin, Northern Illinois, Maryland, LSU, Pittsburgh, and NC State and was a quarterbacks coach for Indiana. Throughout his long career, he has amassed significant fortune and fame. This article delves into his net worth, career highlights, and personal life.

How Much is Matt Canada Net Worth?

As of 2023, Matt Canada’s net worth is estimated to be ranging from $1.5 million to $5 million. These figures reflect his successful career as a coach, primarily in the NFL.

A significant portion of his wealth stems from his role as an offensive coordinator for various NFL teams. Notably, while working with the Pittsburgh Steelers, he signed a contract in January 2021.

Matt Canada Early Life and Education

On January 19, 1972, Matt Canada was born. Canada went to New Palestine, Indiana’s New Palestine High School. He started for two seasons as the starting quarterback, throwing for 1,736 yards and winning 20 games for his squad. Canada subsequently enrolled in Indiana University Bloomington, where, in his sophomore year, he worked as a student assistant for the football team.

How Did Matt Canada Became Famous?

Early stops in Matt’s coaching career were at Northern Illinois, Indiana, Butler, and Wisconsin. Canada was crucial in helping Northern Illinois win the MAC championship in 2011 and in helping Wisconsin go to the Big Ten Championship Game in 2012.

Under head coach Dave Doeren, Matt joined NC State in 2013 as offensive coordinator. Canada has previously worked at Northern Illinois under Doeren in 2011. Shortly after interviewing for Tennessee’s offensive coordinator position, Canada agreed to a new three-year deal with NC State following the 2014 campaign. Afterward, after the 2015 season, NC State fired him.

Matt joined Pitt in 2016 as their offensive coordinator. He was recognized as one of the five finalists for the Broyles Award, which is given to the best assistant coach in college football each year, in recognition of his work during the 2016 campaign.

Matt was hired by LSU on December 14, 2016, to serve as the quarterbacks coach and offensive coordinator. He served in those capacities during the 2017 campaign, then on January 5, 2018, Canada and LSU reached an amicable agreement to split.

Canada consented to take over as offensive coordinator at the University of Maryland on January 22, 2018. Following the passing of offensive lineman Jordan McNair, D.J. Durkin was placed on administrative leave, and Canada was named the Maryland Terrapins’ interim head coach on August 11, 2018.

On November 17, 2018, Maryland lost 52–51 in overtime but was on the verge of defeating Ohio State, which was ranked #3. With a 5-7 record at the end of the season, Maryland was unable to earn a bowl spot.

Canada lost his position as head coach of the Maryland football team after Mike Locksley was appointed as the team’s permanent head coach. Scottie Montgomery took over as offensive coordinator after Canada.

The Pittsburgh Steelers hired Canada on January 15, 2020, to teach their quarterback. Canada succeeded Randy Fichtner as the Steelers’ offensive coordinator on January 25, 2021.

Canada has come under fire during his time in Pittsburgh, mostly because of his playcalling. This has led to the Steeler’s offensive ranking in the lower half of the league ever since Canada was appointed. The Steelers fired Canada on November 21, 2023.
