Where is Deandre Ayton From?

Deandre Ayton, a prominent figure in the National Basketball Association (NBA), was born on July 23, 1998, in Nassau, Bahamas, making him 25 years old. He is a talented center who was the first overall pick in the 2018 NBA Draft by the Phoenix Suns. Ayton's early life and career are a testament to his dedication and passion for basketball.

Early Life and Origin

Deandre Ayton hails from Nassau, the capital and largest city of the Bahamas. His parents, Alvin and Andrea Ayton, raised him in this beautiful island nation. Notably, Ayton's father is of Nigerian descent, and his mother has Jamaican and Bahamian heritage. This diverse cultural background has played a significant role in shaping Ayton's identity and has been a source of inspiration for him throughout his career.

High School Basketball

Deandre Ayton's basketball journey started to gain momentum when he began playing high school basketball. College Basketball

Professional Career
