Black Bird: viewers saying same thing about Taron Egertons Apple TV drama

Have you started the much-anticipated new series yet?

    Apple TV has done it again with their new true-crime thriller as viewers have taken to Twitter to heap praise on the new series, which stars Taron Egerton as a convicted drug dealer trying to have his sentence lifted by coaxing a confession out of another inmate, who is a suspected serial killer.

Another victory for this curation of excellence made by Apple TV. Amazing, two killer episodes, one master class," another added: "The first two episodes of #BlackBird are excellent and absolutely riveting. A slow burn, but one that already has me so glued to the screen and intrigued to see where it goes. Feels like this is bound to be career-best for both Egerton & Hauser."

    The true-crime show has been well received
