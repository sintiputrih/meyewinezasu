Miss Universe: Who Is Antonia Figueroa? Age and Wikipedia, Bio and Parents

Antonia Figueroa is a model, judoka, and beauty queen from Chile competing for Miss Universe 2021.

Antonia Figueroa enjoys playing instruments, dancing, and singing, and she was a MasterChef Chile contestant who graduated with honors in Environmental Civil Engineering.

Antonia survived an earthquake and tsunami in her hometown of Coquimbo in 2015, and she has dedicated herself to actively participating in numerous military missions to help her city and its residents move forward.

She founded Volunteers for Children in 2020, where she recruits people willing to educate for free. Various community members, including the Israeli ambassador to Chile, have donated to her cause.

Who Is Antonia Figueroa? Her Wikipedia & Bio

Antonia Figueroa is engaged in her community, having founded a volunteer group for children to assist disadvantaged youngsters protected by the government.

In the fourth season of the gastronomic television program MasterChef Chile, she competed against 19 other contestants in 2019.

On August 27, 2016, she competed in Miss World Chile 2016 and won the crown, representing Santiago against 14 other contestants.

She participated in Miss World 2016 in Oxon Hill, Maryland, the United States, on December 18, 2016, representing Chile against 116 other competitors. 

Antonia didn’t get a spot on the show, but she placed in the Top 10 in the talent competition.

She represented Coquimbo in Miss Universe Chile 2021, competing against 16 other contestants in San Fernando and winning the crown. She will represent Chile at Miss Universe 2021 in Eilat, Israel, as Miss Universe Chile.

How Old is Antonia Figueroa? Her Age

Antonia Figueroa was born on January 16, 1995, in the Pintana municipality of Santiago de Chile. She lives in Coquimbo, a city known for its civil engineering. 

Figueroa is a woman who has known how to use her time wisely from a very young age. She autonomously learned to cultivate wisdom for the future.

At the age of 5, the model learned how to play the violin, and without teachers or classes, she has already learned four languages.

She stood out in various sports disciplines such as boxing, basketball, and surfing during her youth. Still, taekwondo has given her the most satisfaction, being a regional champion and currently holding a black belt. She has always been a multifaceted woman.
