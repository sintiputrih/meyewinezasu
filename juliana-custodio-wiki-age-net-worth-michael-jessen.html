Breakups are hard, but it gets better as time passes on. Juliana Custodio, who split with her husband Michael Jessen in late 2021 was already recovering from her pending divorce. Keep reading this Juliana Custodio Wiki to learn more about her.

Is Michael Jessen Still Julia Custodio’s Husband?

Technically, yes, as of November 2021, Michael Jessen was still Julia Custodio’s husband. The two had yet to finalize their divorce then.

Their love story was the talk among the 90 Day Fiancé fans when it was at its peak. The first time, they appeared in season 7, many accused Juliana of being a gold digger. However, the rest appreciated her kind-hearted nature, which even managed to befriend Michael’s ex-wife Sarah.

The couple then went on to get engaged in 2019, and the same year, on October 12, the duo tied the knot in an intimate wedding ceremony.

Unfortunately, two years after their marriage, Michael announced their split exactly on their second wedding anniversary. “Today is only our second wedding anniversary and, sadly, our last,” he wrote over IG.

Michael then followed it with a few words of kindness about Julia and went on to blame the coronavirus pandemic for leaving them both “drained.” Also, he admitted that he “did not maintain the strength of mind and the fortitude to be the husband” that Julia wanted him to be. 

“All along, I failed to recognize that you were there for me,” Michael added.

As for Julia’s take on the matter, she shared that she “needed” to leave as she wasn’t herself anymore. She felt more like a maid, forced to take care of Michael, his two children, his ex-wife, and his ex-wife’s new husband.

It seems that Juliana stood up for herself, and knew she had equal worth. She got out of the marriage to asset her value and for that, she had become an inspiration to many.

However, though Juliana is no longer with Michael, she was still protective of his children, Max and Cece. She urged people not to harass them to get more details about their split.
