Can you move into a new construction house before closing?

Answer

Re: Would you move in before you close on a new construction? no, you should not move in before closing. You do not own the property until then and in this credit market and with underwriting rules changing on a weekly basis, moving in early could be disaterous.

Besides, is it legal to move into a house before closing?

Moving in before the closing date is also known as taking early possession of the property. It’s generally not feasible to move in early unless the seller has already vacated the property. However, many real estate agents will discourage the practise of early possession.

The contract terms will determine when you can move in after closing. In some cases, it will be immediately after the closing appointment.
