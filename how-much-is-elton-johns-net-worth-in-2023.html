Who is Elton John?

Being the son of Stanley Dwight, and Sheila Eileen Dwight, Elton John whose birth name is Reginald Kenneth Dwight was born on March 25 1947 in Pinner, Middlesex, England.

He gained his education from P inner Wood Junior School, Reddiford School, and Pinner County Grammar School. However, he left his studies before A-Level examinations to pursue a music career.He started playing piano at an early age, and at the age of 11, he won a junior scholarship to the Royal Academy of Music. Such were his abilities that he started composing music at an early age. Elton studied at the Royal Academy of Music in London for five years.
