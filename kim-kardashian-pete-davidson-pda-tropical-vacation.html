Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson Show PDA on Tropical Vacation

Things are still heating up between Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson.

Today, the SKIMS mogul shared new photos on Instagram from a tropical getaway she embarked on with the former Saturday Night Live star. The pictures show the couple loved up while posing for the camera in the water and going for a ride in a kayak. The two also made sure to coordinate their swimwear, with Kardashian in a black triangle bikini and Davidson in black board shorts (and, of course, it doesn't hurt that they also twinned with platinum-blond hair).

"Beach for 2," the reality TV star captioned the post.

Kardashian and Davidson have been going strong since they first went public with their romance last November.

On Hulu's The Kardashians, the mom of four has also gotten increasingly comfortable talking about their relationship. On a recent episode, she gushed over how they've been progressing.

"Pete and I have been dating for a few months. We're doing really, really good," she said during a confessional interview. "Pete said, 'I'm gonna grow on you. Just wait.' He knew. He's like, 'I give it four months in and you're gonna be obsessed.' And I was like, 'Okay. We'll see.'"

She added that she wants to keep her and Davidson's relationship relatively "drama-free."

"I never want the person that I'm dating to be subjected to public scrutiny just because we're dating," she continued. "He knows what he's getting himself into, obviously, but I'm trying to minimize any tabloid drama."
